How to export from Zoom
  1. Log in to Zoom on the web
  2. Click Reports in the left side panel
  3. Click Meeting and webinar history
  4. Select your date range to find your webinar
  5. Click on the webinar you want to analyse
  6. Click the blue number in the Participants column
  7. Click Export (top right) — do not filter
  8. Upload the CSV here
Don't see the export option? You may need account admin access. Ask your operations team to enable reporting under Account Management → Reports.
How to get your Zoom transcript

Zoom transcripts are generated automatically when cloud recording with audio transcription is enabled.

  1. Log in to Zoom on the web
  2. Go to Recordings in the left panel
  3. Find your webinar recording and click on it
  4. Look for a .vtt or Audio Transcript file
  5. Download and upload it here

Don't see a transcript file?

Ask your operations team to enable Audio transcript under Settings → Recording → Cloud recording.

Accepted formats: .vtt, .txt, .srt
