How to get your Zoom transcript

Zoom transcripts are generated automatically when cloud recording with audio transcription is enabled. Follow these steps:

  1. Log in to Zoom on the web
  2. Go to Recordings in the left panel
  3. Find your webinar recording and click on it
  4. Look for a .vtt or Audio Transcript file in the list
  5. Download it and upload it here

⚠️ Don't see a transcript file?

Transcription may be locked by your account admin. Ask your operations team to enable Audio transcript under Settings → Recording → Cloud recording. Once enabled, all future recordings will auto-generate a transcript.

Accepted formats: .vtt, .txt, .srt
